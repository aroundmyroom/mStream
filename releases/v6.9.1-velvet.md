## Custom tooltip system fix

The `#tip-box` custom tooltip system converts `title` attributes to `data-tip` at init time. However, i18n's `translatePage()` sets `title` attributes *after* init, meaning those translated titles were never picked up — browsers showed their native tooltip instead.

Fix: the `MutationObserver` now watches for `title` **attribute changes** (not just added nodes), so any `title` set after page load is immediately converted to `data-tip` and shown in the styled tooltip.

This affects all player control buttons with `data-i18n-attr="title"`: play, pause, next, prev, shuffle, repeat, expand Now Playing, EQ, Auto-DJ, sleep timer, and more.
