# v6.12.9-velvet — April 2026 — Hydration Queue Limit Fix

## Fix: Artist hydration "Queue All" fails with 400 for large libraries

**Symptom**: Clicking "Queue All" in the admin Artists panel returned HTTP 400 with the message `"limit" must be less than or equal to 9999` for users with more than 9 999 artists missing images.

**Root cause**: The "Queue All" button passes `counts.missing` directly as the `limit` parameter. The Joi schema on both hydration endpoints capped `limit` at `9999`, which is exceeded by any library with 10 000+ unimaged artists.

**Fix**: Raised the Joi `max` for `limit` from `9999` to `100000` in both endpoints:

- `POST /api/v1/admin/artists/hydration-seed`
- `POST /api/v1/admin/artists/hydrate-tadb-noimage`
