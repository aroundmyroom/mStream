# v6.14.2-velvet — April 2026 — Butterchurn Brand Overlay: Fireworks, Nebula & Text

## Overview

This release adds a full animated brand overlay to the Butterchurn/Milkdrop visualiser fullscreen mode. When the **aroundmyroom - mStream Velvet** preset is active, a transparent canvas sits above the visualiser and renders music-reactive fireworks, a pulsing nebula background, and a three-line text signature — all without hiding the Milkdrop animation underneath.

---

## New features

### Music-reactive fireworks

Rockets launch from the bottom of the screen on every bass beat and explode into coloured sparks. Everything scales with the music:

- **Beat onset detection** — fast EMA vs slow EMA on the sub-bass band; fires a rocket each time bass jumps 40% above its rolling average
- **Explosion variety** — 12–35 main sparks, 1–2 expanding flash rings, and a glitter burst of tiny slow-fading dots per explosion
- **Multi-palette colours** — each rocket randomly picks a colour palette: mono (tight hue band), duo (complementary pair), rainbow (full spectrum), or triadic (3 hues 120° apart)
- **Energy scaling** — harder hits produce larger explosions with extra rockets; sub-bass, mid, and treble each drive their own visual channel

### Nebula background

Three transparent radial gradients breathe with the music behind the fireworks:

- Bass → warm glow in the lower-left
- Treble → cool shimmer in the upper-right
- Mid → soft bloom at the centre

Uses `clearRect` + fully transparent fills — Milkdrop stays 100% visible through the overlay.

### Brand text signature

A three-line text block sits in the lower third of the screen:

```
Thank you for using
mStream Velvet
AroundMyRoom
```

- Animated hue-cycling gradient colouring
- Glow shadow that pulses with bass intensity
- Static position — no movement, no bounce
